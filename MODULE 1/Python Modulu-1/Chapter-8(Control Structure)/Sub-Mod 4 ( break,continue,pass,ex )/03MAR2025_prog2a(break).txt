break  statement
--------------------
1) while  cond:
	    stmt1
	    if  cond:
	          break  
	    stmt2
    # End  of  loop
    stmt3

2) What  does  break  statement  do ? --->  Moves  the  control  out  of  loop

3) What  is  the  next  statement  executed  after  break ?  --->  Statement  outside  the  loop

4) What  are  skipped  due  to  break ?  --->  Remaining  statements  of  the  loop 
											and 
								       also  remaining  iterations  of  loop 

5) Who  can  use  break ?  ---> for  loop  and  while  loop

6) Can  break  be  used  without  a  loop ?  --->  No

7) In  other  words,  break  can  not  be  used  without  a  loop

8) Is  Break  valid ?   --->  No  becoz  break  is  a  keyword  and  hence  it  should  be  in  lowercase  

9) When  is  break  recommended ?   --->  To  move  out  of  loop
