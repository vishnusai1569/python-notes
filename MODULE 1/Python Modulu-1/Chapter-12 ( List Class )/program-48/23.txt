# reverse()  method  demo  program (Home  work)
a = [10 , 20 , 15 , 18]
print(a) #  [10,20,15,18]
a . reverse()
print(a) #  [18,15,20,10]



'''
reverse()  method
---------------------
1) What  does  reverse()  method  do ?  --->  Reverses  elements  of  list

2) Where  are  the  results  stored ?  ---> In  the  same  list  replacing  existing  elements  (List  is  mutable)
'''
